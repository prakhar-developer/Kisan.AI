KISAN.AI
Kisan.AI is an integrated solution offers inventory management and a marketplace for selling crops. The platform leverages AI for precise seed and fertilizer recommendations, and employs IoT for smart irrigation management. Machine learning models detect plant diseases, while AR and OpenCV facilitate efficient inventory tracking. It also includes weather prediction features, simple Google authentication, and mobile notifications for critical alerts such as flood risks, inventory status, and irrigation needs.
